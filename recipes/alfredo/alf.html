Chicken Alfredo

Everyone loves creamy chicken alfredo! This recipe is better than Olive Gardens!

Ingredients

    16 oz pasta 1 pound chicken breast (diced)
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 3/4 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pint heavy whipping cream
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • 1 clove garlic
